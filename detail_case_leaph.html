Case Study : Leaph Street
Designing a new legal cannabis delivery app in New York City
Stakeholder: leaph street
Site: http:leaphstreet
Duration: September 2022 - March 2023
PROJECT DESCRIPTION

My goal is to design and develop a legal cannabis delivery app that provides a seamless and secure user experience for customers in need of legal cannabis products. The app will allow customers to browse, purchase, and receive legal cannabis products directly to their door, while complying with all state and local regulations.

PROJECT GOALS

The goal is to create a user friendly interface that allows far easy navigation and the purchase of legal cannabis and to ensure the app is fully compliant with all municipal laws and regulations. To provide a secure and reliable platform for customers to make transactions and receive deliveries. To develop a brand identity that accurately represents the values and goals of the company.

PROJECT RISK

Legal compliance: ensure the app is compliant with local and state laws and regulations. User adoption: ensure the app is user-friendly and appeals to target audience. Technical issues: ensure the app functions properly and is reliable. Competition: ensures the app stands out from other legal cannabis delivery apps in the market.

PROJECT CONCLUSION

The successful completion of this project will result in a legal cannabis delivery app that provides a seamless and secure user experience for customers in need of legal cannabis products. The app will help ensure that customers have access to legal and regulated products, while providing a safe and convenient platform for delivery.
